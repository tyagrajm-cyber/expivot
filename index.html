Platform Terms & Conditions
Effective 1 August 2026 · Expivot Platform · Version 1.0
1. Platform use and eligibility

Expivot is a professional marketplace platform for mid-career independent consultants in India. By registering, you confirm you are at least 18 years of age, legally eligible to work as an independent contractor in India, and have the professional experience to offer consulting services.

2. Independent contractor status

You are an independent professional, not an employee of Expivot. Expivot acts solely as a marketplace connector between consultants and project buyers. Nothing in this agreement creates an employment, partnership, or agency relationship between you and Expivot.

3. Financial readiness acknowledgement

By using this platform, you confirm that you have independently assessed your personal financial readiness before transitioning to independent consulting. Expivot does not guarantee income, project availability, or continuity of work. The platform provides tools and connections — outcomes depend on your own effort, expertise, and market conditions. You acknowledge that financial readiness is entirely your personal responsibility and Expivot bears no liability for income loss or financial hardship arising from your use of the platform.

4. Data and privacy

Your profile data, CV content, and consulting offer generated on the platform are owned by you. Expivot uses this data solely to match you with relevant project buyers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Profile information is shared with buyers only after you go live and only to facilitate project matching.

5. Escrow and payment terms

For each accepted project, 50% of the agreed project fee is deposited into Expivot escrow before work begins. Escrow is released within 24 hours of milestone approval by both parties. Expivot charges a platform commission on each completed project. Direct payment arrangements that bypass the platform are a violation of these terms.

6. Dispute resolution

In the event of a dispute between a consultant and a buyer, both parties agree to first attempt resolution through Expivot's mediation process. Expivot's decision in mediation is binding for amounts up to ₹5,00,000. Disputes above this threshold may be referred to arbitration under Indian law.

7. Platform fee and commission

Expivot charges a commission on each project completed through the platform. Commission rates are displayed at the time of project acceptance and may vary by project size and type. Subscription plans, if applicable, are billed monthly and are non-refundable.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate their use of the platform at any time. Termination does not affect obligations under active projects or the Expivot Compact signed for ongoing engagements. Expivot reserves the right to suspend or remove accounts that violate these terms.
